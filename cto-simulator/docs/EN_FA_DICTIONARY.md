# English → Persian Dictionary (CTO Simulator Codebase)

Comprehensive dictionary of **hard** and **medium-hard** English words and phrases extracted from the CTO Simulator codebase (glossary, briefings, levels, feedback, and UI). Intended for learners and translators.

---

## How to use

- **Hard**: Technical or domain-specific terms that often require explanation.
- **Medium-hard**: Compound phrases or terms that are clear in context but benefit from a precise Persian equivalent.
